Important PC Addresses to Potentially Break on:
0xbfc0'0000 -- Start of Bios
0xbfc0'0150 -- Bios Initialization routine start
0xbfc0'0250 -- Initializing cache
0xbfc0'0278 -- After Zeroing places in RAM (not sure what its doing)
0xbfc0'029c -- Zeroing out more places in RAM
0xbfc0'0328 -- After zeroing out more places in RAM
